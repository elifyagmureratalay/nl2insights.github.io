This project introduces a three-stage architecture that transforms natural language queries into structured database queries and produces concise, informative summaries.

ðŸ“„ Abstract

As the volume of digital information continues to grow, intuitive and scalable systems are needed for querying and interpreting large datasets. This project presents a modular architecture that (1) converts natural language input into structured queries, (2) executes those queries on OpenSearch or other databases, and (3) summarizes top results using LLMs. It avoids full database embedding and allows integration with systems like PostgreSQL, MongoDB, or Elasticsearch.
